Our restaurant is open from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM, seven days a week. We are closed on major holidays.

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary restrictions. Please inform our staff of any specific dietary needs or restrictions when placing your order.

~Do you have a children's menu?

Yes, we have a children's menu with a variety of dishes suitable for younger guests. Please ask our staff for more information

~How do you prepare your dishes?

Our chefs use traditional recipes and innovative techniques to prepare each dish. We believe in the art of cooking and strive to bring out the best in every ingredient.

~ How do you ensure food safety and hygiene?

We adhere to strict food safety and hygiene standards. Our kitchen staff is trained in proper food handling and preparation techniques. We also regularly sanitize our premises and equipment.

Yes, we have a selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes on our menu. Please check our menu for specific options.

~. Do you offer any special promotions or discounts?

Yes, we occasionally offer special promotions and discounts. Please check our website or contact us for the most current deals and promotions.

~. Is there parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking available. Please inform our staff if you require assistance with parking.

~. Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for private events, corporate functions, and special occasions. Please contact us for more details and to place an order.
